The Colors of Golden Retrievers

Golden Retrievers can be found in 3 three general colors: light golden, golden, and dark golden. The color of the coat is hereditary and the entire litter will have the same color coat as the parents.

Light Golden Retrievers have a coat that is a color slightly darker than completely white. This color can also often be called cream. The Light Golden coat color is most often associated with the English Golden Retrievers. The most famous light golden retriever is “liberty the first dog”, this was American President Gerald R. Ford’s family dog, one of the most famous golden retrievers in America’s history.

The most well-known image of the golden retriever is one that has a golden colored coat, as that’s where the name came from.
There are many famous Golden Retrievers with the typical golden colored coat. Air Buddy, seen in the “Air Bud” movie and “Full House” series is an extremely famous stunt dog. His fame went far enough to even get him nominated for a Kid’s Choice Award for “Favorite Animal Star”. Another famous one is named Tucker Budzyn whom has over 3 million followers from social media. The final one is named Pinkie. Pinkie won the Best in Breed for the Westminster Dog Show and mothered three orphaned Tiger cubs.

The final color is a Dark Golden Color . This color breed can often be mistaken for red or copper, however it is actually just a deeper tone of golden yellow.
