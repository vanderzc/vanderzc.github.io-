Three Types of Golden Retrievers

  • American Golden Retriever
    1. The American stands out for being the most friendly and iconic type of Golden Retriever.
    2. This type of Golden is typically smaller than the others. They have lankier and less muscular bodies compared to the other two.
    3. The American Golden Retriever has a much darker colored coat compared to the English and Canadian Goldens.
    4. The American has a very specific gait that appears much more smooth and powerful than most dogs.
    5. The most apparent factor is their eyes, which are triangular shaped. Their head should not have any prominent features such as a large forehead or sunken eyes.
    6. Their hindquarters are the most muscular part of them and they have a pelvic bone that sits at a 30-degree angle.
  • English Golden Retriever
    1. The English Golden Retrievers were first bred in Scotland and are also known as English Cream Golden Retrievers or British Golden Retrievers
    2. They are most similar to American Golden Retriever, but there are a few main physical differences
    3. They have a broad skull and chiseled mussel. These things are due to their original breeding being done with the Tweed Water Spaniel.
    4. They have the lightest coat out of all three types of Goldens.
    5. English Golden Retrievers have round eye shapes and darker eye colors.
  • Canadian Golden Retriever
    1. They were originally bred in Canada.
    2. The hair length on the Canadian Golden Retriever is usually much shorter, thinner, and denser, than the other two.
    3. The Canadian Golden Retriever is the tallest type of Golden, standing as many as 2 inches taller.
